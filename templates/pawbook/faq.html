Frequently Asked Questions


What is Pawbook and how does it work?

Pawbook website provides a totally free to use advertising platform for private individuals responsible breeders and rescue centers can list their adverts on.Potential buyers or adopters of pets can use the website to make initial contact with the advertisers. Our website also aims to educate and give the necessary advice for potential pet oweners.

How to deal safely on Pawbook

1. Find out more about the Seller

2. Always deal within Pawbook

3. Be prompt

4. Have decent common sense

5. Deal at places you are comfortable with

How do I make purchases on Pawbook?

To buy an item, simply select the listing you are interested in and engage the buyer. In your conversation with the buyer, you can start to ask the buyer questions and request for more photos to deal with confidence

How do I sell on Pawbook?

It's easy. All you have to do is to make a post, update the necessary fields and a photo of the pet you wish to list on sale for and you are done.
